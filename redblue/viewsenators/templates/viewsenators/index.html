FAQ

How do I make a custom map for my own cause?
Select which senators you want your friends to contact from this link.

How do I include multiple causes on the same map?
There isn't a good UI for this yet, but you can comma-separate multiple IDs in the URL.

Can I search directly on facebook instead of using this site?
Not really - you'll get garbage if you search "My friends who live in California or live in Arizona" on Facebook.
If you use the links on site, the Facebook search term will look the same, but it will actually work.

Can this work for my representative as well?
Not yet, due to the complex boundaries caused by gerrymandering.
